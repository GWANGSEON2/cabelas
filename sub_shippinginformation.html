Additional Details

Orders placed on holidays, weekend days, or after 4 p.m. Eastern Time on business days will delay shipment. Shipping charges are subject to change without notice.

How Do You Ship Internationally?

Merchandise is shipped from the United States. The majority of in-stock items will ship within 48 business hours (this excludes weekends and US holidays). In some cases, items may ship separately. International shipping charges is 45% of your merchandise total or a minimum of $40.00 USD, whichever is greater. Additional shipping charges may apply for oversized items as indicated in the catalog or Internet site. We cannot bill your personal carrier account for shipping charges. International orders are shipped with a delivery time frame of 7-10 business days once processed. Local customs officials may require inspection of imported parcels and assess appropriate import duties and taxes. Import duties and taxes are not collected at the time of your order and will be the responsibility of the customer at the time of delivery. Please contact your local Customs Office for an estimate of these fees.

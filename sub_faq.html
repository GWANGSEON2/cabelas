HOW TO SUBSCRIBE

You may open a subscription at the time you select the participating item when shopping online at cabelas. com and basspro.com. You can select the timing for delivery and the quantity of items to be shipped to you periodically on the product description page. You must have an open Cabela’s or Bass Pro Shops account in order to participate. If you do not have an existing Cabela’s or Bass Pro Shops account, you will be given the opportunity to open an account at checkout. You are required to provide your name, email address, a password of your choice, your billing and shipping address, and information about a valid payment card. You are responsible for keeping this information up to date and to make appropriate changes by logging into your account. You may not open a subscription as a guest.

CUSTOMIZING YOUR SUBSCRIPTION

You may choose to receive your purchase every week, every 2 weeks, or every month. You may also choose the quantity you purchase through your subscription. Make your selections on the product description page in our online store or log into your account and select "Product Subscription."

CHANGING YOUR SUBSCRIPTION

You may change the frequency of your delivery and the quantity of subscription items to be purchased and delivered by logging into your account and selecting "Subscription." Changes must be made at least 3 days before your next shipment in order to take effect. Changes made within 3 days of your next shipment will take effect after that shipment.

RECURRING PAYMENT

You agree to purchase the quantity of items you have selected at the intervals you have chosen (weekly, bi-weekly, monthly) and to be charged automatically for each purchase on a recurring basis until you cancel subject to the Cancellation Policy. We will endeavor to send you a reminder email at least 5 days before payment is due to the email address listed in your account. The charge to your payment card will include the price of the product ordered plus any applicable taxes and shipping charges. The price will depend on the quantity of the item you ordered and the current online price for the items you have selected. You are responsible for providing a valid payment card at the time you enroll and for keeping a valid payment card on file in your account.

MINIMUM PURCHASE

In order to start a subscription, you must agree to purchase at least 1 item once a month. After your first order, you may cancel your subscription at any time, subject to the Cancellation Policy below. There is no charge for enrolling in a subscription. You only pay for the product ordered plus applicable taxes and shipping charges.

SUBSCRIPTION TERM

Your subscription will continue until you cancel. Please see the Cancellation Policy below.

HOW TO CANCEL

You may cancel your subscription at any time by logging into your account, selecting "Product Subscription," and then selecting "Cancel." You must cancel at least 3 days before your scheduled shipment in order to avoid being charged for the scheduled product shipment.

CANCELLATION POLICY

You may cancel your subscription after purchasing 1 subscription delivery. You must cancel at least 3 days before your next scheduled shipment to avoid being charged for and receiving that shipment. If you cancel within 3 days of your next scheduled shipment, you are responsible for paying for the product shipped in that delivery.

CHANGES TO PRICES

You may cancel your subscription after purchasing 1 subscription delivery. You must cancel at least 3 days before your next scheduled shipment to avoid being charged for and receiving that shipment. If you cancel within 3 days of your next scheduled shipment, you are responsible for paying for the product shipped in that delivery.

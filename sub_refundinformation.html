What is your Return Policy?

Return Policy 

In most cases, if the item was purchased through the catalog or online, you may return it to us for an exchange or refund either by mail or at the store within 60 days of purchase with valid proof of purchase. We will accept items returned after 60 days on an inspection basis only. You may return your items to either company, regardless of whether you purchased from Bass Pro Shops or Cabela's.:

Exclusions:

Online orders placed using PayPal as a payment method can be returned to a retail store, but we can only exchange or refund as a gift card. Purchases made at Sunglass Hut locations within our retail stores must be returned to those locations. Merchandise purchased from cabelas.ca or at a Cabela’s retail location in Canada must be returned to Cabela’s Canada. Gasoline-powered items and drones are ineligible for return if they have been used, show signs of use, or if oil or fuel has been added.

Returns to a Store Without a Receipt or Proof of Purchase

Items returned without a receipt will be processed at the lowest sale price plus applicable sales tax. Such items may be exchanged for the same product or returned for merchandise credit in the form of a gift card. A valid government photo ID may be requested. We reserve the right to limit or decline returns and exchanges without valid proof of purchase.

Beyond 60 Days from the Date of Purchase

All electronics and non-Bass Pro brand rods and reels that are covered by the manufacturer's warranty should be returned to the manufacturer for replacement or repair after 60 days. For more warranty

All Bass Pro Shops and Cabela's brand rods have a 1-year warranty, and the following Rods have a ten (10) year warranty: Johnny Morris Platinum Signature, Johnny Morris Signature Series, Johnny Morris CarbonLite 2.0, and Johnny Morris Patriot.

Bass Pro Shops and Cabela's branded boots, shoes, and waders carry a one (1) year warranty against manufacturer defects. This is a limited warranty and does not cover normal wear and tear.
